About Us

MidaDevs is born when three technology enthusiasts with innovative ideas come together. Each of them possesses unique skills and shares the vision of creating a comprehensive web development company, encompassing areas such as marketing, SEO, UX/UI design, and website creation.

Maurizio Abatantuono, an experienced and visionary entrepreneur, contributed the main idea that sparked the creation of the company. United by their determination, MidaDevs was born, merging their talents and knowledge to become a leading force in the digital world.

The mission of MidaDevs is clear: to provide cutting-edge digital solutions that exceed their clients' expectations. Their vision is to become a globally recognized company, setting standards of excellence and making a lasting impact in the web development industry.

MidaDevs offers personalized services ranging from website design and development to digital marketing strategies, search engine optimization, and intuitive user experience design.
